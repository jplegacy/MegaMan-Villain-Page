Bass

Eventually, Bass was made; Who was built in a very similar fashion to Megaman. Bass also known as Forte was Mega man's soon to be Rival. He was made from a research conducted on Mega Man with the intention of surpassing his power.

Protoman

Even though Dr. Light completed Proto Man, which was the first robot with feelings. Proto Man had a defect in his Solar Reactor, and he left Dr. Light's lab fearing to be changed. He wandered around until he eventually stumbled across Dr. Wily. He modified Protoman, giving him a new nuclear core and additional equipment.

Zero

Zero was made to be far more greater than both Mega man and Bass. Zero was eventually dubbed Wily's "Greatest Masterpiece"; But Zero had one big flaw in his cognitive program, he was violent and disobeyed instructions. Because of this, Wily decided to seal him in a capsule.
